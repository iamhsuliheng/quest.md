# QUEST.md

A semantic specification for project documents.

Any project — writing a book, organizing an event, launching a
product, running a campaign — can be described as a quest: you are
trying to get somewhere, you are somewhere right now, and you have
a path between the two.

QUEST.md gives that structure a name.

## The structure

A QUEST.md has two layers and five sections.

**Strategic layer** — why this quest exists
- `Crisis` — the event that made this quest necessary
- `Resolution` — the chosen direction in response

**Tactical layer** — how the quest unfolds
- `Destination` — the imaginable scene of completion
- `Current Situation` — where you are right now
- `Route` — the imagined path, and its constraints
- `Fellowship` — who is on this journey
- `Journal` — a record of how things change

## The core idea

A QUEST.md holds two imaginations and the reality between them.

**Destination** is your imagination of where you are going.
**Route** is your imagination of how you will get there.
**Current Situation** is the reality you are navigating through.

Both imaginations are expected to change under pressure from
reality. The Journal records that history.

## Getting started

Copy [QUEST.md](./QUEST.md) into your project. Fill in what you
know. Leave blank what you don't. A document with only a Crisis and
a Destination is already a valid QUEST.md.
